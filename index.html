Renters' Rights Act comes into force 1 May 2026. Licence renewals require fully current documentation. Missing or outdated certificates are the most common cause of delays and penalties.

Why landlords pay for this

Having your certificates is not the same as knowing they are all valid, correctly dated, and properly organised for a council inspection. Many landlords find at renewal stage that something has quietly lapsed — an EICR that expired at a change of tenancy, a gas certificate covering the wrong appliances, or an EPC rating that is no longer compliant.

One missing document can delay your licence renewal, trigger a Civil Penalty Notice of up to £30,000, or result in a Rent Repayment Order from your tenants.
